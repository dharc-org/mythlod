mythLOD context

mythLOD project has been founded in the context of PON - IDEHA (FICLIT department, University of Bologna) and has been developed by /DH.arc Research Center in collaboration with FRAMELab (University of Bologna)

mythLOD input data

mythLOD Knowledge Base is the result of the data cleaning and conversion process of Mythologiae Collection.
Mythologiae Collection has been developed by FRAMELab.
mythLOD dataset contains Mytholgiae data util June 2020.
